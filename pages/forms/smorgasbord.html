Who can register as a stakeholder

You will be notified whether your organisation fits with the definition for being a stakeholder for the project(s) you select on this form.

Respondents

As a party to the World Health Organisation’s Framework Convention on Tobacco Control, the United Kingdom has an obligation to protect the development of public health policy from the commercial and vested interests of the tobacco industry. Tobacco industry organisations (for example, tobacco companies, those who speak for them or are funded by them) registering to participate in the NICE guideline process will automatically be registered as respondents in the process.

Confidentiality agreement:

As part of the Institute’s pre-publication process, information may be disclosed to stakeholders which is confidential (Confidential Information). To register your organisation as a stakeholder you must read and agree to the confidentiality agreement, on behalf of your stakeholder organisation.
